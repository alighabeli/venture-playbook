# Principles — Overview

## Objective

Define the foundational principles that govern all venture creation activities within the Venture Playbook OS.

These principles apply across every stage, capability, workflow, decision, and AI interaction.

They serve as system-wide constraints rather than stage-specific guidance.

---

## Scope

This stage defines:

- Core venture creation principles
- Decision-making principles
- Validation principles
- Execution principles
- Human-AI collaboration principles
- Complexity management principles
- Common anti-patterns
- Principle review mechanisms

This stage does not define:

- Stage-specific activities
- Deliverables
- Templates
- Venture-specific decisions

---

## Why This Stage Exists

Most venture failures originate from flawed assumptions, weak decisions, poor validation, premature execution, or uncontrolled complexity.

The purpose of this stage is to establish the operating principles that reduce these failure modes before execution begins.

---

## Relationship to Other Stages

The Principles stage precedes all execution stages.

Its outputs become constraints for:

- Vision
- Problem
- Validation
- Product
- Domain Model
- Architecture
- MVP
- Launch
- Growth

and all subsequent stages.

---

### 03-Validation-Principles

### 04-Execution-Principles

---

### 05-AI-Collaboration-Principles

---

### 06-Complexity-Principles

Defines how rigor scales with venture complexity.

---

### 07-Anti-Patterns

Defines common venture-building failure patterns.

---

### 08-Principles-Checklist

Defines review criteria for principle compliance.

---

## Outputs

The outputs of this stage are:

- Venture creation principles
- Decision principles
- Validation principles
- Execution principles
- AI collaboration principles
- Complexity principles
- Principle review checklist

---

## Exit Criteria

This stage is complete when:

- All principle categories are defined
- No principle conflicts exist
- Human-AI responsibilities are clear
- Complexity scaling rules are defined
- Principle compliance can be reviewed consistently

---

## Final Principle

Before defining a venture, define the principles that govern venture creation.

Without principles, execution becomes inconsistent.
